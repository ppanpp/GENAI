Types of Image Generator

Image Generative AI can be broadly categorised into three types. They can be used individually or in combination. All three types are often involved if we want to get an image that meets our expectations.

Text2Image

By leveraging natural language processing and image generation algorithms, Text2image bridges the gap between language and visuals, enabling users to create images directly from written content.

Style Transfer (Image2Image)

Using deep learning algorithms, Style Transfer alters the artistic style of an image to mimic the characteristics of another image or artistic style.

Image Upsampling

Image Upsampling improves the clarity and quality of images by generating additional pixels to enhance details and sharpness, resulting in higher-resolution images with improved visual fidelity.
