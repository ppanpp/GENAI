Midjourney generates images from natural language descriptions, called prompts, similar to OpenAI's DALL-E and Stability AI's Stable Diffusion. It is one of the technologies of the AI boom.

Stable Diffusion

Stable Diffusion is a deep learning, text-to-image model released in 2022 based on diffusion techniques. It is primarily used to generate detailed images conditioned on text descriptions, though it can also be applied to other tasks such as inpainting, outpainting, and generating image-to-image translations guided by a text prompt.

DreamStudio

DreamStudio is an online creative tool to generate images with Al. Made by the world'sleading open source generative Al company, Stability.ai.

Sound Engineers

Suno

Suno is a music generation model launched by the AI startup Suno. Regardless of whether the user has music theory knowledge, simply inputting simple prompts such as music style, genre, lyrics content, timbre, etc., and waiting a few seconds, can quickly generate a 2-minute music piece with lyrics and beats.

Stable Audio 2.0

The renowned open-source large-scale model platform Stability.ai has officially released the audio model Stable Audio 2.0 on its website. This version supports users in generating various types of high-quality music through text or audio, with a duration of up to 3 minutes at 44.1kHz.

Lovo.ai

Lovo.ai is an AI-based voice generator and text-to-speech platform. It is powerful and easy to use, capable of generating voices similar to real human voices.
